About us

IBW is a Lesotho company that operates under the supervision of co-founder Kenneth. The company started in 2024 with a capital of M100000. During his journey on sourcing funds, Kenneth partnered with Shadrack, who joined as a second CEO of the company. The mandate of IBW is to recycle computer parts ; mainly the RAM( Random Access Memory),HDs(hard Drives) and components of the motherboard. In 2025, the company penetrated the market and became one of the pioneers of electronic recycling int the Southern Region of Africa. The growth of IBW attracted investors, parners and clients outside of Lesotho.
